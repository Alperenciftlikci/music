Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Music AI, and tap Cancel. The subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I get a refund?

Refund requests are handled directly by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the Music AI charge.

How do I delete my data?

Either tap Settings → Delete All Data in the app, or email us with the subject "Delete my data" and we will erase everything tied to your anonymous identifier within 7 days.

I'm a Pro subscriber but the app says I'm not.

Tap Restore Purchases on the paywall — this re-syncs Apple's receipt with our servers. If the issue persists, please email us your subscription start date so we can look up your account on RevenueCat.

Can I use generated songs commercially?

Pro subscribers retain commercial-use rights to their generated songs, subject to TemPolor's terms governing AI-generated audio. Free-tier songs are for personal use only.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings → Delete All Data in the app. This wipes every song, playlist, and the anonymous identifier tied to your device — irreversibly. You can also email aimusicgenerator.app@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my data".
